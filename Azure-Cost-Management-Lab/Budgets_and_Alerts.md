# Step 2: Creating Budgets and Alerts in Azure

## **Budget Configuration**
- **Budget Name:** MonthlyCostControl
- **Scope:** Subscription-wide (Azure for Students)
- **Monthly Limit:** $50
- **Alert Thresholds:**
  - **25% Alert:** $13 (Email notification)
  - **45% Alert:** $23 (Email notification)
